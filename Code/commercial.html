COMMERCIAL

 
Commercial Services:
> Land Development Planning (FEMA Flood Zone permitting)
> Letter of map revisions for construction or fill (LOMR)
> Reduction of Existing Flood Insurance through FEMA rezoning
> Specialized Engineering support for City & Water District permitting in FEMA zones

 

Whether your property is existing or in design, we can assist with your FEMA zoning issues. FEMA's laws can affect your property's development potential, or mandate excessive insurance policies on your existing buildings. Our staff of Engineers are available to support your case for development or post-construction review. Please contact us via email so we can help.

Please note that client's with existing mandated flood insurance policies have a potential of Zero out of pocket expense, but time plays a major factor so please contact us as soon as possible.
